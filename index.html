Audio Examples

For five audio watermarking methods (AudioSeal, WavMark, Timbre-Watermark, MaskMark, and Collaborative), we provide examples of watermark removal transformations that leverage low-bitrate neural audio codecs and neural denoisers. In each row, we provide the original (un-watermarked) audio, audio watermarked with each method, and watermarked audio with a transformation applied.
